# Reports and Printing
The Print menu (**Imprimir**) provides access to the main reports. Reports are sent to the configured printer.

## Acumulador de Ventas (Sales Accumulator)

Prints a summary of sales totals for a selected period, including:

- Total calls by type (local, DDN, DDI, cellular, border, special).
- Subtotals, tax amounts, and grand total.
- Toll-free calls count and amount.
- Turn number and operator name.

Period options: **Day**, **Week**, **Month**, **Year**.

**Path:** Imprimir \> Acumulador de Ventas

## Historial de Cabina (Booth History)

Shows the transaction history for a selected booth. Calls are listed with date, time, duration, destination, and cost. Individual receipts can be reprinted from this view.

**Path:** Imprimir \> Historial de Cabina

## Recibo de Turno (Turn Receipt)

Prints a consolidated summary receipt for the current turn, listing all calls and totals. Typically printed at end-of-shift for supervisor verification and cash reconciliation.

**Path:** Imprimir \> Recibo de Turno

## Reimprimir Recibo (Reprint Receipt)

Reprints any receipt stored in the current turn database by receipt number. Enter the receipt number in the dialog and click **Aceptar**.

**Path:** Imprimir \> Reimprimir Recibo
